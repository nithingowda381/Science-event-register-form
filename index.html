Math Puzzle and Poster Making Challenge

Math Puzzle Challenge Rules and Regulations:

  • Group Composition: Each group must consist of two participants.
  • Electronic Gadgets: The use of electronic gadgets (ELC) is strictly prohibited during the puzzle challenge.
  • Time Limit: The puzzle challenge has a duration of 30 minutes. Groups must complete the puzzle within this time frame.

Poster Making Competition Rules and Regulations:

  • Group Composition: Each group must consist of two participants.
  • Electronic Gadgets: The use of electronic gadgets (ELC) is strictly prohibited during the poster making competition.
  • Topic: The poster must be related to a physics-related topic chosen by the participants.
